Privacy Policy for SafeCheckerBot

Effective Date: 03/04/2025

1. Introduction

SafeCheckerBot ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our bot to log in to Fortnite (Epic Games) and retrieve your inventory.

2. Information We Collect

When you use SafeCheckerBot, we collect the following information:

  • Epic Games Account Information: We request access to your Fortnite account through Epic Games OAuth 2.0 authentication. This includes a unique code that allows the bot to retrieve your inventory (skins, pickaxes, items, etc.) associated with your account.
  • Access Token: After successful login, we store an access token to communicate with the Epic Games API. This token is used to fetch your Fortnite inventory.
  • Images of Inventory: The bot will send images of your inventory as requested.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Authenticate users with Epic Games.
  • Retrieve and display your Fortnite inventory (including skins, pickaxes, items) via images.
  • We do not use or share your information for any other purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypting the access tokens. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your collected data.
  • Request the deletion of your data.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the official bot communication channels.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Effective Date" at the top of the policy.
